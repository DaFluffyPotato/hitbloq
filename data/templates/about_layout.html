About

Hitbloq is a competitive ranking service for Beat Saber. Our goal is to provide a leaderboard and ranking system that anybody can use to create their own ranked ladders with a map pool of their choice. At the moment, Hitbloq is effectively an extension to Score Saber since we run off of their data. Any scores on Score Saber can be imported into Hitbloq. A large part of Hitbloq is the Hitbloq Discord. The Discord server is a space for suggestions and discussion while also being the location the voting and ranking processes for the main map pool take place.

About

Hitbloq is a competitive ranking service for Beat Saber. Our goal is to provide a leaderboard and ranking system that anybody can use to create their own ranked ladders with a map pool of their choice. At the moment, Hitbloq is effectively an extension to Score Saber since we run off of their data. Any scores on Score Saber can be imported into Hitbloq.

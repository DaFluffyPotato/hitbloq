Hitbloq is a competitive Beat Saber service with
endless map pools where anything is rankable.

Map Pools

Map pools are a collection of maps that have an associated ranked ladder so you can compete on them. The pool owners have full control over the difficulty ratings, the reward curve, and more!

Hitbloq Mod

Hitbloq has a mod that provides in-game leaderboards and automatic profile refreshes. Since Hitbloq runs off of ScoreSaber and BeatLeader data, the Hitbloq mod isn't required.

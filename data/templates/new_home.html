Map Pools

Map pools are a collection of maps that have an associated ranked ladder so you can compete on them. The pool owners have full control over the difficulty ratings, the reward curve, and more!

Using The Website

Star ratings, CR (competitive rating) rewards, and leaderboards are specific to the map pool you're viewing. Switch your selected map pool on the map pools page. Your current selected map pool can be seen on the bottom left.
